## Decoration

The decoration was the final step before completing our final project. Our goal was to recreate the scene as faithfully as possible, with a particular focus on the light wall. For the rest of the setting, we decided to keep it minimal and only included a small, handmade cardboard sofa to suggest the living room environment. <br> 

We built the overall setting using a shoebox as the base and designed the project around it. Materials used included wrapping paper and markers for the wall, additional paper and cardboard for decorations, and leftover pieces of the shoebox to create the sofa. <br> 

While the set could certainly be enhanced further, our main focus was on implementing the technical aspects as intended and presenting the project in a clear and visually understandable way, staying as close as possible to the series. Ultimately, we were very satisfied with the result we achieved. <br> 

Once the decoration was complete, we moved on to filming the final [demo video](Demo-Video/link-to-the-demo-video.md), which showcases the project in its entirety.
