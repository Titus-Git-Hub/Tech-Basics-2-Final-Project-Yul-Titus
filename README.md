The objective of the final project was to choose a task that was appropriately challenging. One that would allow us to demonstrate 
the skills we had acquired, while still remaining feasible to complete within the given timeframe. <br>

For our project, we recreated the iconic “Alphabet Wall” from the Netflix series Stranger Things using our Arduino kit... 

-------

**-> Background: Stranger Things** <br>

_Stranger Things is a popular Netflix series created by Matt Duffer and Ross Duffer. The show first premiered in 2016 and has since gained a large global audience across multiple seasons. It combines elements of science fiction, mystery, horror, and 1980s coming-of-age storytelling._ <br>

_The story takes place in the small fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, where a series of unexplained and supernatural events begin to occur._ <br>

_At the beginning of the series, viewers are introduced to a group of four close friends, including Will Byers, who regularly spend their evenings playing Dungeons & Dragons together. After one of these game nights, the boys ride their bikes home. During the trip back, they split up and Will suddenly disappears under mysterious circumstances._ <br>

_The following day, Will cannot be found anywhere. As the news spreads, the entire town begins searching for him and trying to understand what happened. The situation becomes increasingly tense and confusing, especially for Will’s single mother, Joyce Byers, who is desperately worried about her son._ <br>

_Soon after Will’s disappearance, Joyce begins noticing strange phenomena in her house. The lights start flickering and behaving erratically, as if something is trying to communicate with her. Although the situation seems unbelievable, Joyce becomes convinced that these signals are connected to Will._ <br>

_Believing that the lights might allow her to reach her son, she begins attempting to communicate with him through them. Unknown to most of the town, Will is actually trapped in a parallel dimension known as the Upside Down, a dark reflection of the real world that exists alongside their own._ <br>

_Understanding how Will ended up there and what exactly is happening in Hawkins is part of the mystery that unfolds throughout the series. However, this brief overview should already provide enough context to understand the idea behind our project: recreating the iconic alphabet light wall that Joyce uses to communicate with Will._ <br>
